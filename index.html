Game Description

Welcome to Night at the Valley, an enchanting idle tower defense RPG where you’ll step into the shoes of Moo and Lulu, two brave guardians of Emerald Village. Mysterious forces threaten the peace of the land — it’s up to you to build and upgrade your defenses, cast magical spells, and uncover the truth behind the invading creatures.

Whether you’re an idle game fan or a strategy master, Night at the Valley offers a rich, rewarding experience packed with charm, mystery, and magical warfare. The village needs you. Are you ready to defend?
